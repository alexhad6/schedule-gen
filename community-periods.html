Adding Community Periods

  1. Open Google Calendar on a computer.
  2. Copy: https://calendar.google.com/calendar/ical/friendsseminary.org_bj1lodmosc6s7rv90tha3em8q4@group.calendar.google.com/public/basic.ics
  3. On the left side, click > Add by URL
  4. Paste the link.
  5. Click Add Calendar.

    If the calendar still does not show up on your iPad, please visit calendar.google.com/calendar/syncselect to make sure it is syncing.
