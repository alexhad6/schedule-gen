Uploading Instructions

Step 1

Go to calendar.google.com and log in.

Step 2

Create a new calendar to upload your schedule to.

Step 3

Go to your calendar settings.

Step 4

Select “Calenders” at the top.

Step 5

Click “Import calendar” at the bottom.

Step 6

Choose your schedule file and press “Import”.

Step 7

From here, you can log in to your Google account on an iPad and view your schedule in the Calendar app.
